Engineered for Precision

The RB26DETT engine represents the pinnacle of Japanese engineering. This twin-turbocharged 2.6-liter inline-6 produces 276 horsepower in stock form, but its robust design allows for incredible tuning potential.

Combined with the advanced ATTESA E-TS all-wheel-drive system, the R34 GT-R delivers exceptional traction and control in all conditions.

R34 LEGACY

The final chapter of the classic GT-R era (1999-2002)

The Final Classic

The R34 GT-R represents the pinnacle of the classic Skyline GT-R lineage. With its refined RB26DETT engine, advanced ATTESA E-TS AWD system, and iconic design, it became the most sought-after Japanese sports car.

Featured in countless movies and video games, the R34 achieved legendary status that transcends automotive enthusiasts, becoming a cultural icon worldwide.
